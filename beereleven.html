FRIVILLIGBAJEREN

beers

35 DKK

ABV: 4.5% Our dear brewer, Rune, has produced a beautiful, clear, light Pale Ale with a fruity scent of peach and pineapple as well as low bitterness. This is the second batch (2.0) of this easy-drinking Pale Ale, and the recipe has changed a bit. Frivilligbajeren combines Lagerens maltsøme and Pale Alen's hop bite for a beer with a meaningful focus: In collaboration with Ingerfair, Frivilligbajeren is developed by a grateful heart over all kinds of voluntary work and efforts, and the sales profit from the flag bearer here goes to create jobs for people in marginalized groups and courses for volunteer leaders.
